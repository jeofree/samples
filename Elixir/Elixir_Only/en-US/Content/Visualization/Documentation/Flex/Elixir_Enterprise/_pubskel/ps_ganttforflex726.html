Coordinate representation

The location of a point can be of the form loc:offset. loc can be either a longitude or lati-tude or the key of a map feature. In the case of loc representing a key, the barycenter of the corresponding map feature will be used. A possible offset is added to determine the final location.
left=”0.12” top=”45.3”  0.12 longitude, 45.3 latitude
left=”FRA” top=”FRA” barycenter of France used for left and right
left=”0.13:20” 0.12 longitude + 20 pixel offset
Longitude or latitude can be expressed in decimal degrees or in DMS, Degree minute second format. 
verticalCenter=”11D 19M 0S W”             11 degrees 19 minutes 0 second West
upperLeftLatitude="90°00'00"N"                       in mxml file, can’t use “ for seconds
upperLeftLatitude="90°00'00"N"                       can use ° and ‘
upperLeftLatitude="90° 00' 00S N"                    can mix DMS symbols
