Diagram

In IBMÂ® Diagram, a diagram is an instance of the Diagram class that is able to display nodes and links, creating their representation based on business data.
Diagrams are used to show the relationships between entities in a system. The entities are called nodes and the relationships are called links. Nodes are specified through the nodeDataProvider property and links are specified through the linkDataProvider property. Using the Diagram class, objects can be laid out using graph layout algorithms or can have their position specified according to properties in the business data objects. Diagrams also provide support for select, move, zoom, pan, and scroll operations and for customized interactions such as editing and drill-down.
